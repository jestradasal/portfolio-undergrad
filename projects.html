Demographic Variables Affecting North Carolina SAT Scores

CS 216: Everything Data, Spring 2017, Final Project

Gathered SAT score data from across North Carolina by county. We then used general demographic variables such as race, income or percent of students receiving free/reduced lunch. Our data found that most of these had a strong correlation to SAT scores, giving way to reinforce what many other studies have found.

Robot Car

MITES, Class of 2015, Electronics Final Project

Used Raspberry Pi and Python to program a robot car controlled with a joystick using a breadboard and LED lights. We attached sensors around the car in order to be able to navigate using only LED lights that turned on when their respective sensor ways triggered.
